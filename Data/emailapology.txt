Here's an example of an email apology:

Subject: Apology for [Mistake/Incident]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to express my sincerest apologies for [mistake/incident] that occurred on [date]. I understand that my actions/inactions caused [undesirable outcome] and for that, I am truly sorry.

Please know that it was not my intention to [cause harm/convenience]. I take full responsibility for my mistake and assure you that I am taking immediate action to prevent such incidents in the future.

If there is anything I can do to make it right, please let me know. I value our [relationship/business] and appreciate your understanding in this matter.

Once again, I apologize for my mistake and any inconvenience it may have caused.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Example Use Cases:**

* Apologizing for a delayed response to an email
* Apologizing for a mistake in a project or task
* Apologizing for poor customer service
* Apologizing for a missed deadline

**Tips:**

* Be sincere and genuine in your apology
* Acknowledge your mistake and take responsibility
* Offer a solution or make amends
* Keep the tone professional and polite
* Proofread your email for grammar and spelling errors before sending.
